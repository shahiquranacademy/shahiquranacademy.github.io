Dear parents,

Every child deserves a strong Islamic foundation. If you want your son or daughter to recite the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and confidence, we offer a structured, child-friendly learning system that is proven to work.

Our teaching method keeps children engaged and motivated, ensuring they learn with consistency, clarity, and discipline—without feeling overwhelmed.

At Shahi Online Quran Academy, children learn Quran with Tajweed, القاعدة (Qaida), handwriting practice, Makhraj training, and beautiful recitation—Insha’Allah.

We offer both group and one-to-one sessions, allowing parents to choose what suits their child best. Every class is designed with patience, compassion, and focus on your child’s growth.

Give your child the gift of Quran today—an investment that lasts a lifetime.
